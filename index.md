## Introduction

We begin with a story everyone knows: The wealthy man who owns a building but carries a battered old Samsung phone. Is he cheap? Or is he a genius? This book is the answer. It is not about paranoia. It is about strategy. It is about learning the difference between what you can't control and what you absolutely can. This is the art of keeping.
